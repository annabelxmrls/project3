The Victoria's Secret Fashion Show was a previously annual event sponsored by the lingerie brand, Victoria's Secret. The fashion show showcases and promotes their brand's products in glamourous settings. This event is also highly known for it's well-known models and music performances by famous artists.

The video below is presenting one of the music performances of the Victoria's Secret Fashion Show in 2012 in New York City. While most people may recognized the singer, Rihanna, they would not know the names of the models walking down the runway.
